When I'm not helping teams be more productive while maintaining a healthy work-life balance, I'm doing the following things.

Home Life

Although originally from the Rio Grande Valley, I have been in Houston, TX for the last decade. I like to go out with my friends and experience everything this metropolis has to offer us. The flavors of Houston have inspired me to spend a lot of time in the kitchen. While I love cooking all kinds of food, I am always trying to perfect the regional Mexican food of my hometown of McAllen, TX. Currently my signature dish is homemade green chile enchiladas.

Recreation

Whenever I have some time to myself you can find me voraciously consuming media from my library. I have a large collection of Stephen King and other classic horror novels. I usually have a goal of 25-30 books read by the end of the year. I love watching movies at the theater; I go several times a month. My passion for these stories has inspired me to work towards creating stories and experiences of mine that have teeth so deep they won't let go of you.
